Plughub Email Cleaner - Privacy Policy

Effective: 29 November 2025

1. Google user data we access

  • Gmail message headers (sender, recipient, subject, labels, thread and message IDs) to identify newsletters, promotions, and other mail that can be cleaned.
  • Gmail message bodies and snippets when you run the cleaner so we can detect content types (promotional, social, bulk, unsubscribe links) and show you what will be acted on.
  • Gmail labels and folder structure in order to apply actions you choose (label, mark as read, archive, trash, or unsubscribe).
  • Your Google account email address to display inside the add-on and to confirm your subscription or support requests.

2. Data we do not access

  • No Google Drive, Calendar, Contacts, Photos, Docs, or other Google data outside Gmail.
  • No location data, device data, or Google search history.
  • No use of your Gmail data for advertising, profiling, or data brokerage.

3. How we use your Gmail data

  • Analyze message headers and bodies in-memory to classify emails and recommend cleanup actions.
  • Show you the messages identified for cleanup so you can approve or skip them.
  • Perform only the actions you select (label, mark read, archive, move to trash, or unsubscribe) through the Gmail API.
  • Maintain minimal operational logs limited to timestamps, action types, counts, and error codes; these logs never include message content or subject lines.
  • Use your email address to verify eligibility, process payments, and respond to support inquiries.

4. Storage and retention

Email content remains in your Gmail account; we do not copy or store Gmail message bodies on Plughub servers. Processing happens transiently while we run the action you asked for. Any operational logs that exclude message content are retained only as long as needed for support, security, or legal compliance.

5. Sharing and selling

We do not sell or rent your data. We do not share Gmail data with third parties except (a) service providers who enable payments or hosting and only to the extent necessary to provide the service, or (b) when required by law.

6. Security

We use TLS for data in transit and restrict access to support personnel with a need to know. OAuth tokens are handled by Google Add-on infrastructure; we do not store them on our servers. Protect your Google account credentials and revoke access if you suspect misuse.

7. Your choices and control

  • You can revoke the add-on's permissions anytime in your Google Account settings or by uninstalling the add-on.
  • You can undo or delete actions in Gmail using Google's native controls.
  • For data questions or deletion requests for information we store outside Gmail (e.g., billing records), contact plughubgadgets@gmail.com.

8. Children's data

The service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes

We will post updates on this page and adjust the effective date when the policy changes. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised terms.
