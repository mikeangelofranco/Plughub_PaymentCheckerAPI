Plughub Email Cleaner – Privacy Policy

Effective: 20 November 2025

1. Data We Access

Plughub Email Cleaner requests only the Gmail scopes necessary to scan and manage email content for cleaning. We do not request or use broader Google permissions unrelated to the add-on’s functions.

2. How We Use Data

  • Identify and organize unwanted or low-priority email content.
  • Apply user-selected cleaning actions within Gmail.
  • Maintain operational logs solely for support and security.

3. What We Don’t Do

  • No selling or renting of your email data.
  • No sharing of your data with third parties except as required to operate the service or comply with law.
  • No use of your email content for advertising.

4. Storage & Retention

We retain minimal operational logs for troubleshooting and security, and only for as long as needed. Email content is processed transiently to deliver the cleaning features and is not stored long-term.

5. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data in transit and at rest. You are responsible for safeguarding your Google account credentials and access tokens.

6. Third-Party Services

Payments are processed via our payment provider. They receive only the data necessary to complete transactions, subject to their own privacy terms.

7. Your Choices

  • You can revoke the add-on’s permissions at any time via your Google account.
  • You may uninstall the add-on to stop all processing.
  • For data questions or deletion requests, contact us at plughubgadgets@gmail.com.

8. Children’s Data

The service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have, contact us to remove it.

9. Changes

We may update this policy with notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
